    About Us

    Bahrain World Trade Centre, Manama, Bahrain

    Delivering Iconic Building Faades

    a

    Alutech Aluminium And Glass is an emerging faade solutions provider established in 2023, bringing fresh innovation and expertise to support the delivery of iconic building faades from inception through to final installation.

    As one of the newest architectural envelope and glazing contractors from the Middle East, we specialise in the design, engineering, manufacturing, project management and installation of bespoke architectural faades worldwide.

    We have been perfecting the art and science of faade design, manufacture and installation for over half a century.

    Our Global Achievements

    Over the past five decades, we have engineered over 500+ world-class buildingfaades including the Burj Al Arab, the Dubai International Airport and the Burj Khalifa.These projects exemplify the quality and commitment that we bring to our projects, and the depth of our expertise in crafting high-performance faades.

    Our Mission

    Bringing Visions to Life

    We design, manufacture and install bespoke and customized building envelopes for landmark projects in all building sectors.

    We offer our technical expertise and design innovation to bring the vision of the worlds leading building designers, contractors and developers to life.

    Our mission is to lead the faade industry by innovating customized faade solutions that can achieve the highest levels of performance while being delivered on time and on budget.

    Shaping The Future

    Alutech Aluminium And Glass has been a pioneer in the faade industry with an extensive list of major past achievements. The company remains firmly focused on shaping the future of faade design.

    We support environmentally friendly practices and sustainable use of resources. We are considerate in all of our relationships with clients, subcontractors and our staff.

    Embedding Innovation

    Innovation is embedded in every stage of our process to ensure that the resulting faade solution not only meets the needs of the present but also anticipates future environmental and performance needs.
