    Commitment, Quality, Sustainability & Innovation

    Alutech Aluminium And Glass – A growing architectural building enveloping and glazing contractor in the UAE. As a young company established in 2023, we specialise in the design, engineering, manufacturing, project management and installation of bespoke architectural facades.

    About Us

    Alutech Aluminium And Glass is a UAE-based company specializing in premium aluminium and glass solutions. With our expertise in aluminium windows & doors, curtain walls, structural glazing and other architectural elements, we deliver high-quality products and installations to meet our clients' needs.

    As a young and dynamic company established in 2023, we focus on innovative solutions including automatic sliding systems, frameless shower enclosures, spider glass installations, and fire-rated products - bringing modern architectural visions to life through expert craftsmanship and attention to detail.

    Based in the UAE, Alutech Aluminium And Glass is committed to excellence in design, manufacturing and installation of architectural aluminium and glass products that enhance both residential and commercial spaces.
